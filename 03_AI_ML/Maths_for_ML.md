# Maths is the base of AI and ML.

## Learning Maths is essential as it develops logic and required critical thinking to make AI and ML come into action. Here are some of the best resources I found online. These resources include books, lectures, playlists, courses, etc. Most of them are free.



### 1. Courses
- Scaler Free Maths for ML Course - https://www.scaler.com/topics/course/mathematics-for-machine-learning-free-course/
- Master the Mathematics Behind AI and Unlock Your Potential by Deeplearning - http://deeplearning.ai/courses/mathematics-for-machine-learning-and-data-science-specialization/
- Maths for ML by MIT Open Courseware - https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/18-657-mathematics-of-machine-learning-fall-2015/


### 2. YouTube Lectures/Playlists
- Linear Algebra for ML by Dr Jon Krohn - https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLRDl2inPrWQW1QSWhBU0ki-jq_uElkh2a
- Linear Algebra by FreeCodeCamp - https://youtu.be/QCPJ0VdpM00?si=MAfCKHOvmvZGHgbb


### 3. Books and Online Materials
- Mathematics for Machine Learning by Marc Peter Deisenroth, A. Aldo Faisal, Cheng Soon Ong - https://mml-book.github.io/book/mml-book.pdf
- Linear Algebra by Gilbert Strang - https://rksmvv.ac.in/wp-content/uploads/2021/04/Gilbert_Strang_Linear_Algebra_and_Its_Applicatio_230928_225121.pdf
